About me

I'm Aaron Cano (known as rxnwashere on the internet), a young Spanish
computer technician and web development student.

I studied Microinformatic Systems and Networks Vocational Course
for 2 years to become a computer technician.

Due to be an autodidact person, in my free time I make different little projects
that help me to improve my skills as a computer technician and as a programmer
and web development.

That's the reason behind the creation of Ron Lib, a place where I can upload
my class projects and my own ones, and allow everyone to download them,
always giving credits to me.
